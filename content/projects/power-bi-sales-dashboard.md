## Overview

This project focuses on presenting sales performance through a clean executive dashboard experience in Power BI.

## What I Worked On

- Prepared the dataset for dashboarding with Power Query.
- Planned KPI cards, trend views, and regional breakdowns.
- Designed interactive filters for time period and geography analysis.
- Structured measures for growth, totals, and comparison views.

## Key Outcomes

- Built a layout aimed at fast decision-making for non-technical stakeholders.
- Made it easier to compare revenue performance over time.
- Used drill-down interactions to move from summary metrics to detailed views.
- Created a polished reporting experience that complements deeper data analysis.
