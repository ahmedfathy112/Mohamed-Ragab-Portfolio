## Overview

This project explores Kaggle's annual machine learning and data science survey across multiple years to understand how the field changed over time.

## What I Worked On

- Cleaned and standardized survey exports from multiple years.
- Built a reusable analysis workflow in Python with Pandas and NumPy.
- Compared salary, experience, geography, and tooling adoption trends.
- Visualized changes in Python usage, compensation, and respondent distribution.

## Key Outcomes

- Identified strong salary gaps across countries and experience levels.
- Showed that Python adoption kept increasing year over year.
- Highlighted where compensation growth starts to flatten after more years of experience.
- Turned a large, messy survey dataset into a clear business-facing story.
