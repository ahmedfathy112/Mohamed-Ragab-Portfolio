## Overview

This project started with a messy retail sales dataset and turned it into a reliable source for exploratory analysis and business reporting.

## What I Worked On

- Fixed missing values, inconsistent types, and duplicated records.
- Built a repeatable cleaning flow for product, sales, and customer fields.
- Analyzed category performance, regional demand, and seasonal revenue movement.
- Created visual summaries that made the findings easy to communicate.

## Key Outcomes

- Reduced data quality issues before analysis.
- Exposed a clear Q4 sales spike across top-performing categories.
- Highlighted the product groups contributing the largest share of annual revenue.
- Produced charts and summaries that could support business decisions quickly.
